Who I Am

My name is Dana Ellerman and I'm a passionate TIGI stylist and fashionista. I've been professionally licensed for over ten years in North Carolina. In that time I have mastered a wide range of hair techniques including cutting, creative colouring and formal styling.

To stay on the forefront of hair and fashion I continually strive to progress my knowledge by studying in New York City at TIGI Soho Academy, Wella The Studio Academy in London, England, Toni & Guy Academy in London, England, Mastering MAC Cosmetics at London Beauty Institute of London, England. I specialize in cutting edge colouring techniques, modern cuts, fashion cuts, balayage color, styling, special occasion hairstyles and waxing.

I aim to give my clients the best. As a TIGI stylist I use TIGI Bedhead and MoroccanOil products.
